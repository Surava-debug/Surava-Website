Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026

Surava Capital ("Surava Capital," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your information when you visit our website or work with us as a client.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Information you provide directly — such as your name, email address, phone number, and any details you share through our contact forms or during advisory engagements.
  • Financial information — for clients, information necessary to provide advisory services, including account balances, income, and goals, collected in accordance with our advisory agreement.
  • Usage data — information automatically collected when you visit our website, such as browser type, pages viewed, and time spent on the site, typically through cookies and analytics tools.

How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our advisory and educational services
  • Respond to inquiries and communicate with you about our services
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations applicable to investment advisers
  • Analyze and improve the content and performance of our website

How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with trusted service providers who assist in operating our business (such as custodians, portfolio management software, and IT providers), and only to the extent necessary for them to perform their functions. We may also disclose information when required by law or regulatory authority.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to understand how visitors use our site and to improve the browsing experience. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Data Security

We maintain administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or destruction.

Your Choices

You may contact us at any time to ask what personal information we hold about you, request corrections, or ask that we delete information we are not required to retain by law or regulation.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
