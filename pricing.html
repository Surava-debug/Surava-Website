Fee FAQ

Common questions about our fees

Do you accept commissions from any product?

No. Surava Capital is fee-only, meaning the only compensation we receive is directly from our clients. We do not accept commissions, referral fees, or revenue sharing from any fund company, insurer, or custodian.

How is the 0.65% fee calculated?

The annual fee is calculated as a percentage of assets under management and billed quarterly in arrears, based on your account's average daily balance for the quarter. The rate may decrease at higher asset levels.

Are there account minimums?

Ongoing Advisory has a recommended minimum of $250,000 in investable assets. Plan Only has no minimum and is a good starting point for those below this threshold.

Can I cancel at any time?

Yes. Our advisory agreements have no lock-in periods or early termination fees. You may cancel at any time, with fees pro-rated to the date of termination.
