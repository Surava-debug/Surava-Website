About Surava Capital

Advice that answers to you — not a parent company.

Surava Capital was founded to close the gap between what people are told about investing and what actually works. We're an independent, fiduciary advisory firm built around education, transparency, and long-term thinking.

Our mission

Make sound financial guidance accessible, understandable, and free of conflicts.

Too many people make major financial decisions based on advice from someone paid to sell them a product. We believe that's backwards. Surava Capital exists to give individuals and families a place to get advice that is built around their goals first — with every recommendation explained in plain language.

We combine one-on-one advisory work with a growing library of free educational resources, so clients leave every conversation understanding not just what to do, but why.

What guides us

Our principles

These commitments shape every recommendation we make and every piece of content we publish.

Fiduciary, always

Every recommendation we make is required, by both policy and standard, to be in your best interest — not ours.

Radical transparency

Our fees, our process, and our reasoning are disclosed up front. No fine print, no surprise charges.

Evidence over emotion

We build portfolios around decades of academic research on diversification, costs, and behavior — not headlines.

Education first

An informed client makes better decisions. We'd rather teach you the "why" than just tell you the "what."

Plain language

If we can't explain a strategy without jargon, we don't recommend it. Clarity is part of the service.

Long-term partnership

We measure success in decades, not quarters — and we're with you through every stage of life.

Our story

How Surava Capital came to be

2010

Founded as an independent practice

Surava Capital began as a small, independent advisory practice focused on fee-only financial planning for families and retirees.

2015

Launch of the Education Hub

We began publishing free guides and tools to help the public understand investing basics, retirement planning, and tax strategy.

2019

Expanded portfolio management

Introduced low-cost, diversified model portfolios built on evidence-based investing principles for clients at every account size.

2023

Reached 1,200+ households advised

Surava Capital's client base grew to over 1,200 individuals and families across the country, all served under a single fiduciary standard.

Today

Independent, growing, and education-led

We continue to operate independently — no proprietary products, no commissions, and a growing library of free resources for the public.
