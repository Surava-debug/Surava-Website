Fee-Only / Fiduciary Standard

References to "fee-only" describe a compensation structure in which an adviser's only source of compensation is fees paid directly by clients, as opposed to commissions or other third-party payments. References to "fiduciary" describe a standard of care requiring an adviser to act in a client's best interest. Actual obligations depend on an adviser's registration status and applicable law.
