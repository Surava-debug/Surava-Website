Services

The advisory services we're building toward.

Our long-term goal is independent, fee-only advice for young Swiss investors. We are not there yet, and we are honest about it. Today, everything we offer is free: market education, macro commentary, and a public trading journal.

Fee-only, never commissions Independent No proprietary products Swiss-focused
Where we are today. Surava Capital is not yet entered in the Swiss adviser register under FIDLEG, so we do not currently provide personal investment advice or portfolio management. The services described below are what we plan to offer once we are properly registered. Until then, our Education Hub, Insights, and Trades journal are free and open to everyone.

Retirement Strategy Planned

Turn a lifetime of savings into a confident retirement income plan.

Retirement in Switzerland is built on three pillars, and how you combine them matters as much as how much you save. The strategy we're building toward focuses on drawing income tax-efficiently across all three.

  • Coordinating AHV (Pillar 1), occupational pension (Pillar 2), and private savings (Pillar 3a)
  • Lump-sum versus pension decisions on Pillar 2 capital
  • Withdrawal sequencing and tax planning across cantons
  • Longevity and healthcare cost considerations
